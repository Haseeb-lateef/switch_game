🎲 Pig Dice Game

Welcome to the Game!

A fun two-player dice rolling game built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

🎮 Game Features:

  • Roll the dice to earn points
  • Avoid rolling a 1 or lose your turn
  • Hold your score to bank points safely
  • First to 20 points wins!
  • Clean, responsive design

How to Play

1. Click "Roll" to roll the dice

2. Click "Hold" to bank your points

3. Avoid rolling a 1 or lose your turn

4. First to 20 wins!
